Drop multiple Prethink data-table CSVs at once (ClassQualityMetrics, MethodQualityMetrics, PackageQualityMetrics, CodeSmells, TestGaps, TestQualityIssues). Each CSV is auto-detected by its column fingerprint, and every visualization whose inputs are present is rendered. Human-readable column headers (e.g. Class name, Cyclomatic complexity) are accepted in addition to camelCase.
